Registry Office

Annette and Lewis will officially be married on October 31st, in Plymouth registry office. There is a very tight limit on the number of witnesses to this service and we apologies for anyone who really wanted to watch a stern legal reading and Lewis attempting to remember how to use a pen.

There will be a small gathering and laid back lunch following this service and then Annette will be able to start panic-planning all the last minutes things we needed. We will reach out seperately to people to let them know about the service and lunch, but look forward to celebrating with you all the following day.

Day

  • Please aim to arrive for the ceremony between 12:30 and 1PM on the day
  • The official ceremony will start at 1:30 PM.
  • We will be having a blessing during the ceremony, followed by an afternoon tea, activities, and photos.
  • Location: Bickley Mill, Country Inn & Wedding Venue

Evening

  • If you are attending for the evening only, please arrive from 5:30 PM
  • Dancing, drinking, and merryment to follow. (or else)
  • The cake cutting is aimed at 6:45, and our first dance as husband and wife will be at 7PM.
  • Evening food will be served at 7:30 PM.
  • We'd recommend escorts home or to various hotels to be before midnight, cause there is no way we will still be awake
  • Dress code: Autumnal wedding with optional Halloween flare.
